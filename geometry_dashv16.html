GD Update Changelogs

v1.6 - The UFO Update (Current)

  • Added **Level 12: Theory of Everything** (Insane) presenting dense hazard corridors and anti-gravity switches!
  • Introduced **UFO Gamemode**: Press Space/Click to flap upward like a flappy disc while fighting gravity constraints.
  • Added **Ship Customization**: Explore custom Ships 1-4 with unique unlocks.
  • Added **Cube 25 (Theory of Nothing)** unlocked by clearing Level 12 in Practice Mode.
  • Implemented **Live Customized Icon Previews** directly on level selectors and online map drawers matching your color selections in real time!
  • Added this interactive **News Button** archive panel!

v1.5.5 - The Demon Update

  • Added **Level 11: Clutterfunk** (Insane) featuring rotating Saw Blades and scale shifting.
  • Added **Saw Blades** hazards and **Mini / Normal Size Portals** to shrink or restore player scale.
  • Added canvas-rendered **Demon Difficulty Face** directly modeled off **`image_bf7f5e.png`**.
  • Seeded 5 legendary online Demon stages: *The Nightmare*, *Bloodbath*, *DeCode*, *Nine Circles*, and *Sonic Wave*.

v1.5 - The Blue Orb Update

  • Added **Level 10: XStep** (Insane) introducing extreme reflex tunnels.
  • Added **Blue Jump Pads** and **Blue Orbs** to flip gravity mid-air upon collision.
  • Fully updated **Difficulty faces** based on standard designs in **`image_1a16c4.png`**.
  • Added Cubes 23 & 24.

v1.4 - The Ball Update

  • Added **Level 9: Cycles** (Insane) with deep purple visual grids.
  • Added **Ball Portal & Ball Gamemode** to flip gravity smoothly on any flat surface.
  • Added double-click inventory spec inspect modals.
  • Added Cubes 21 & 22.

v1.3 - Horizontal Mirrors

  • Added **Level 8: Time Machine** (Harder) featuring the **Mirror Portal** that flips screen perspectives!
  • Added search filters in Creator hub.

v1.0 - v1.2 - Release Edition

  • Initial core release featuring Stereo Madness to Jumper official maps, draft constructor editor, practice checkpoints, and retro Web Audio synthesizer sounds.
